The Great Doge Burn

Wat burn?

During the Great Doge Burn anyone can burn some of their DOGE or SHIB and receive a share of newly-minted SDOGE!

Why burn?

At the end of every day (or every 27,000 blocks to be precise), 1 million SDOGE will be distributed proportionally to all burn participants.

How burn?

  1. Send any amount of SDOGE to 0x000000, or SHIB to 0x000000
  2. Check out your spot in the burn pile here
  3. At the end of the day, receive your share of SDOGE!

Wen burn?

We're lighting the burn pile at midnight UTC on August 1st, and it will run for 69 days (27,000 * 69 blocks).
